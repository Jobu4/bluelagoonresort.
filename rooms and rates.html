BLUE LAGOON

ROOMS AND RATES

Blue Lagoon has a collection of spacious Rooms. These are all beautifully spaced out ensuring peace and privacy. Each room has a veranda and en suite bathroom with hot showers, towels and toiletries. Twin, double and triple rooms are available. Cots can be arranged for children at no extra cost. All rooms are air conditioned and beds have mosquito nets. There is a safe for valuables at reception, and laundry service available at a small extra cost.
