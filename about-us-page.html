ABOUT US

OUR STORY

The Blue Lagoon was founded by brilliant young men with a dream of owning a luxurious hotel resort at the beachfront.They envisioned a serene and picturesque location where guests could escape from the hustle and bustle for daily life and indulge in ultimate relaxation. After years of hard work and dedication,they finally made their dream a reality.

IMPACT TO THE
LOCAL COMMUNITY

The investment has also impacted the local community by offering job opportunities to locals, training them in hospitality and customer service . As the hotel became more successful, they invested back into the community, supporting local businesses helping to improve the infrastructure of the area . The founders also started to offer tours of the surrounding villages, highlighting the unique culture and tradition of the area.
